Mobile children’s event planning

About P Kids Events

P Kids Events creates playful, well-organized celebrations for children and families. We help shape the theme, activities, timing, and practical details so hosts can enjoy the day with their guests.

The service model is made explicit because P Kids Events has no fixed party venue.

Your venue, our team

We bring the celebration to you

P Kids Events does not operate from a fixed party location. Our organizers travel to the home, school, event hall, garden, or other client-specified venue listed in the booking request. We confirm access, travel arrangements, setup needs, and availability before the event.
